Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Magenta Arrows

On the Subject of Magenta Arrows

Stop saying “point” for everything. What does that even mean! Point!

Pointity pont point point!

There’s no call for that kind of language, laddie.

On the module there are 4 directional buttons colored magenta, a display screen in the middle and a needy timer on top.

Upon activation, the screen will show two letters, and one of the arrows will turn white.

Reorient the diagram below so that the north arrow is pointing in the same direction as the white arrow on the module.

Press the arrow that contains the first letter, then press the arrow that contains the second letter. You will have 30 seconds to do this.

Pressing an incorrect arrow or running out of time will incur a strike and deactivate the module.
